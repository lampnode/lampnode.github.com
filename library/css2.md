#CSS2 Help Sheet

## Syntax
### External style sheet
	<link href="/path/to/cssfile" rel="stylesheet" type="text/css" media="all" />

### Internal Style
	<style type="text/css">
		select{property:value}
	</style>
### Inline style
	<tag style="property:value" ></tag>
